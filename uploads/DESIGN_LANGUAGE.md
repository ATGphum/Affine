# AFFINE BRAND & DESIGN LANGUAGE

## INTELLIGENCE, COMPOUNDED

**Affine makes intelligence compound.**

Bittensor incentivises an open economy of models, compute, inference, data, environments, agents and specialised capabilities.

But producing intelligence is not the same as accumulating progress.

Without coordination, evaluation and inheritance, distributed capability remains fragmented. Individual outputs may be valuable, yet the wider system repeatedly begins again.

Affine changes that.

It aligns distributed capabilities so that what proves useful becomes the foundation for what comes next.

---

## 0. Doctrine Summary

Affine is the persistent mechanism through which distributed intelligence is:

**evaluated → aligned → composed → inherited**

It is not a single model, a conventional research lab or a passive marketplace.

Models will change. Participants will change. The frontier will change.

Affine is the mechanism that allows advancement to persist across those changes.

**Bittensor is the incentive layer.**

**Affine is the alignment layer.**

Alignment does not mean making every participant identical or requiring shared intentions.

It means creating conditions in which different motives, models and capabilities can produce a collectively useful outcome.

### The Strategic Problem

Decentralisation can generate extraordinary capability while still failing to produce cumulative intelligence.

Without an aligning mechanism:

- resources remain isolated
- competitions produce temporary winners but no lasting inheritance
- useful capabilities fail to compose
- local incentives may not advance the wider system
- progress is repeatedly rediscovered rather than retained

Affine exists to prevent decentralised intelligence from becoming a collection of disconnected commodities.

**Competition creates possibility.**

**Alignment converts possibility into progress.**

Affine does not merely participate in the decentralised intelligence economy.

It completes its logic.

### The Affine Standard

Every major Affine expression should make distributed intelligence becoming cumulative feel visible.

It should show:

- independent actors or fragmented capabilities entering a shared field
- pressure, constraint or evaluation aligning their activity
- weak pathways receding
- useful pathways strengthening
- complementary capabilities composing
- validated progress leaving a trace
- that trace becoming the foundation for what comes next

The system learns, and the visual world remembers.

### Recursive Frontier As Master Grammar

The selected landing field direction is not a one-off aesthetic treatment.

It is the clearest expression of Affine's identity.

Affine's design language should therefore be built around a **Recursive Frontier**: a living but instrumented field where unresolved capabilities enter, encounter shared evaluation pressure, and either recede, compose or become inherited progress.

This is the master visual grammar for the brand.

It applies to:

- full-bleed landing experiences
- product dashboards
- miner metrics
- benchmark tables
- mechanism diagrams
- still social assets
- documentation and manifesto pages

But it does not apply at the same intensity everywhere.

The rule is:

**Translate the frontier; do not copy the hero.**

The landing may show the field directly.

Product pages should show the instrumented consequences of the same field.

Static assets should preserve the same relationships even when nothing moves.

### Recursive Frontier Primitives

Every Affine surface should be composed from these primitives.

| Primitive | Meaning | Moving expression | Static/product expression |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Candidate matter | Independent capabilities entering the system | spectral strands, divergent motion, local variation | rows, traces, challenger marks, candidate lines |
| Evaluation pressure | Shared constraints that make work comparable | bending, compression, thresholds, channels | axes, rubrics, baselines, benchmark frames |
| Interference | Competing or incompatible paths | crossing, phase separation, turbulence | overlap, contested states, split deltas, uncertain labels |
| Reinforcement | Useful paths strengthening | brighter traces, repeated alignment, composed flow | heavier lines, selected rows, improving series, confidence markers |
| Recession | Weak paths failing or losing relevance | dimming, shearing, straying off | faded rows, failed traces, low-alpha history, red/grey states |
| Resolution | A useful path becoming clearer than alternatives | convergence, crystallisation, state change | champion marker, selected threshold, frontier line, winning comparison |
| Inheritance | Validated progress becoming new baseline | gold trace persisting into the next cycle | gold frontier, lineage marker, carried-forward benchmark, retained state |
| Reopening | New possibility emerging from inherited state | fresh spectral matter entering again | challenger queue, next epoch, unresolved slots, open comparison |

If a visual element cannot be mapped to one of these primitives, it is probably decorative.

### Expression Modes

Affine needs one language with different intensities.

| Mode | Primary use | Field intensity | What carries the frontier grammar |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Field Mode | landing, first-impression brand, selected manifesto moments | highest | full-bleed field, display thesis, perimeter evidence |
| Instrumented Field Mode | network overview, frontier summaries, mechanism explainers | medium | charts, threshold diagrams, lineage, compact field bands |
| Product Instrument Mode | miner tools, benchmark tables, filters, detail pages | low | rows, axes, deltas, badges, selection states, micro-traces |
| Archive Mode | history, docs, manifesto, explanatory content | low to medium | strata, retained traces, chapter structure, proof inserts |

The modes are not separate styles.

They are different renderings of the same causal sequence:

**DIVERGE → ALIGN → RESOLVE → INHERIT → REOPEN**

### Mode Selection Rule

Choose the mode from the user's need, not from the designer's desired mood.

| User need | Correct mode | Design responsibility |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Feel what Affine is | Field Mode | make the mechanism emotionally legible |
| Understand the frontier | Instrumented Field Mode | make the field measurable |
| Act inside the system | Product Instrument Mode | make decisions fast, accurate and repeatable |
| Remember or explain the system | Archive Mode | make inheritance and causality durable |

When a screen has multiple needs, the highest-risk need wins.

For example:

- if users must compare miners, Product Instrument Mode wins over atmosphere
- if visitors must first understand the thesis, Field Mode may lead
- if a page explains evaluation mechanics, Instrumented Field Mode should bridge field and evidence
- if a section preserves lineage or doctrine, Archive Mode should calm the surface

This prevents the Recursive Frontier from becoming a mood board.

### Translation Rules

Field Mode may be atmospheric because the field is the subject.

Instrumented Field Mode must keep the field subordinate to evidence.

Product Instrument Mode must remain usable for repeated technical work.

Archive Mode must preserve calm authority and memory.

In all modes:

- gold is inherited validation, not decoration
- spectrum is unresolved candidate matter, not a brand gradient
- motion is consequence, not ambience
- density is allowed when it improves comparison
- stillness is allowed when it preserves evidence
- the Affine mark is an anchor, not the whole explanation

This is the practical bridge between the loved landing aesthetic and the rest of the app.

The app should feel like the landing field became an instrument.

**Affine makes intelligence compound.**

The intelligence is not only in the participants.

It is also in the conditions connecting them.

Affine should not appear as a central intelligence commanding the network from above.

It should feel like the deep structure through which independent activity becomes collectively productive.

The brand should express confidence in systems rather than personalities.

**The mechanism persists.**

**The frontier moves.**

## 2. Role Of Affine

Affine is the alignment layer for distributed intelligence.

It creates the conditions through which independent capabilities can become cumulative progress.

It aligns by:

- defining shared evaluation pressure
- making useful work comparable
- rewarding capability that survives constraint
- allowing complementary strengths to compose
- preserving validated advancement as inherited state
- reopening that state to further divergence

Affine does not depend on every participant being benevolent, coordinated or correct.

It depends on the mechanism being aligned.

Good intentions under poor incentives can produce harmful outcomes.

Self-interested actors under well-designed incentives can produce collective value.

**Alignment matters more than intention.**

## 3. Creative Principle

**Directed Emergence**

Complex intelligence does not always need to be centrally authored.

Under the right constraints, sophisticated structures emerge through local action, pressure, competition, reinforcement, selection and memory.

Rivers form channels through terrain.

Lightning resolves through resistance.

Fungal networks allocate growth toward resources.

Neural pathways strengthen through utility.

Evolution retains successful adaptations without planning the final organism.

These systems are not centrally scripted.

They are also not random.

Their outcomes are shaped by underlying conditions.

Affine applies this principle to machine intelligence.

**Many autonomous actors.**

**One aligned process.**

**Increasingly capable outcomes.**

“Directed Emergence” is the internal creative principle, not necessarily the public tagline.

### Design Translation

The visual system should begin with plurality and resolve into earned structure.

It should feel:

- structured, not controlled
- alive, not biological
- alchemical, not occult
- precious, not luxurious
- complex, not chaotic
- monumental, not loud
- dynamic, not decorative
- natural, not illustrative

## 6. Operating Cycle

The system follows a recurring pattern:

**DIVERGE → ALIGN → RESOLVE → INHERIT → REOPEN**

### Diverge

Multiple actors, models and possibilities enter the system.

Represent through:

- independent trajectories
- fragmented capabilities
- candidate matter
- branching
- separation
- multiple local motives
- unresolved spectral variation

### Align

Shared incentives, constraints and evaluation make activity directionally useful.

Represent through:

- fields of pressure
- thresholds
- channels
- compression
- constraint
- weighted movement
- trajectories bending toward shared conditions

### Resolve

Weak pathways fail. Strong pathways reinforce. Complementary capabilities compose.

Represent through:

- receding failed paths
- reinforcement
- increasing coherence
- convergence
- composition
- crystallisation
- a validated structure emerging

### Inherit

Validated advancement becomes part of the system's new state.

Represent through:

- gold state
- retained trace
- historical strata
- new baseline
- operational memory
- measured advancement

### Reopen

The advanced state produces a new field of possibilities.

Represent through:

- new divergence from the inherited state
- fresh candidate matter
- frontier continuing beyond the frame
- productive instability
- visible non-finality

The system never fully resets.

It remembers.

This cycle should inform Affine's motion, product behaviour and visual storytelling.

## 9. Landing Page Direction

The current frontend makes the full-bleed Frontier Field the hero.

This is correct.

It is also the canonical **Field Mode** expression of the broader Affine system.

The landing does not invent a separate aesthetic.

It reveals the source language that other surfaces must translate with more discipline and density.

The landing page should not require a large central logo, an "inspect frontier" prompt or a modal replay to explain the thesis.

The field itself should carry the emotional and conceptual burden.

### Preferred First-Viewport Model

- small stable Affine lockup in the frame
- full-bleed sublime Frontier Field as the centre
- headline and proposition over the field
- current champion and benchmark evidence at the periphery
- no large central mark
- no click target inside the field
- no "inspect frontier" pill
- no modal layer required to reveal the main thesis

### What The Hero Should Express

The hero should feel like an intelligence frontier being inspected from inside the instrument.

It should show or imply:

- independent candidate matter
- shared field pressure
- directional alignment
- weak pathways receding
- useful pathways reinforcing
- validated gold state
- memory traces
- reopening into the next cycle

The viewer should understand before reading deeply:

**something alive is happening inside a system that can measure it.**

### Evidence In The Frame

The more sublime the field becomes, the more disciplined the periphery must be.

The frame should preserve:

- Affine identity
- navigation
- current champion
- era / epoch
- benchmark values
- coordinates or state labels
- primary action

This keeps awe instrumented.

### What The Hero Should Not Do

Avoid:

- central logo fighting the field
- duplicated field replay in a modal
- "inspect" prompts when the field is already visible
- decorative particles with no system role
- ambient beauty that does not imply alignment
- a sci-fi flythrough with no evaluation logic

If the field is doing the work, let it breathe.

The logo does not need to explain the whole system.

The identity around it carries the richer narrative.

The mark should compress one essential idea:

**Independent forces resolving into a persistent structure capable of producing further advancement.**

It should feel both constructed and discovered.

It may contain tension between:

- divergence and resolution
- plurality and coherence
- stability and transformation
- a persistent mechanism and an evolving internal state

It may draw from alchemical geometry, but it must remain contemporary, recognisable and simple.

The logo should function as the seal of the mechanism.

It is not an illustration of AI, nature or gold.

### Frontend Application

On the current landing page, the logo should operate as a stable lockup in the frame.

It should not compete with the full-bleed Frontier Field.

It should not require a large central appearance to make the brand legible.

Use the mark as:

- identity seal
- navigation anchor
- institutional signature
- favicon / app icon
- small lockup mark

Do not use it as redundant hero content when the field is already expressing the mechanism.

## 11. Motion Language

Motion must reveal cause, consequence and accumulated change.

It is not decoration.

Affine motion should communicate:

- divergence
- alignment
- pressure
- reinforcement
- composition
- resolution
- inheritance
- reopening
- memory

### Mechanical Qualities

- stable intervals
- repeatable cycles
- shared origins
- thresholds
- channels
- grids
- constraints
- measured pacing

### Living Qualities

- local variation
- branching
- delayed response
- path dependency
- reinforcement
- weak paths fading
- useful paths strengthening
- non-identical repetition

### Sublime Field Motion

The landing field may use:

- volumetric depth
- particles
- camera movement
- luminous candidate matter
- trails
- pressure waves
- material crystallisation
- gold resolution moments

But every element must have a system role.

Page-load motion should feel like the mechanism waking up, not like a reveal effect laid on top of the page.
Avoid full-viewport gold flashes. If gold appears on load, it should be restrained, directional and tied to validation or inherited evidence.

### Recursive Frontier Core

The preferred Affine field behaviour is recursive compounding, not a single resolution event.

In Field Mode, this can appear as a full-bleed animated frontier.

In product and static modes, it should appear as charts, traces, thresholds, lineage, queues and state changes.

The system should show:

- unresolved spectral strands entering from the candidate field
- some strands aligning toward an inherited gold trace
- other strands missing, shearing away, dimming or receding
- aligned strands strengthening the gold trace
- the gold trace persisting as the next cycle's inherited baseline
- new spectral strands entering and attempting to converge again

Gold should therefore feel accumulated, not applied.

It may appear as a filament, channel, trace or narrow material condensation, but it should not become:

- a broad gold beam
- a central gold panel
- a decorative glow behind the headline
- a full-field wash
- a trophy object

The successful feeling is:

**possibility repeatedly enters the mechanism; only useful paths become inherited gold.**

This core behaviour is not optional decoration.

It is the visual expression of Affine's product thesis.

Any page may reduce its intensity for usability, but it should not abandon the grammar.

Particles are acceptable when they behave as:

- candidate matter
- memory
- pressure
- failed pathways
- composed capability
- validated state

Particles are not acceptable when they are merely:

- ambient sparkles
- decorative dust
- generic AI energy
- a galaxy background
- a screensaver
- constant RGB confetti

### Symbolic Resolution

Transient symbolic disorder may be used in field-led landing moments when it behaves like base material resolving into language.

This is not a general glitch effect.

It represents:

- unresolved possibility
- symbolic base matter
- evaluation pressure
- transformation into legible capability
- the mechanism converting noise into usable signal

Allowed use:

- the landing display phrase
- capability-word transitions
- brief page-load activation
- desktop or large-screen field contexts
- approved capability words such as `reasoning`, `intelligence`, `deduction`, `abduction` and `cognition`

Rules:

- the final state must always resolve to readable language
- the unresolved phase must be brief
- the phrase must remain causally connected to the field
- mobile should prefer stable readable wording unless the transition is proven not to harm hierarchy
- reduced-motion states must show the resolved word immediately
- symbolic characters must feel mathematical or computational, not occult

Do not use symbolic resolution for:

- product metrics
- tables
- hashes
- navigation
- buttons
- labels required for comprehension
- arbitrary decorative equations
- continuous unreadable text

The effect succeeds when a viewer feels:

**raw symbolic matter has been evaluated into a capability.**

It fails when it feels like:

- terminal hacker styling
- decorative math
- generic AI glitch
- a readability failure
- mystical or occult symbolism

Used for:

- supporting information
- prior eras
- inherited baselines

### Spectrum

Spectrum represents unresolved possibility and active variation.

Use spectrum for:

- candidate states
- divergence

- model comparison
- interference
- active computation
- emerging complexity

Spectrum may appear as:

- lines
- traces
- offsets
- particles
- fibers
- candidate matter

Spectrum must not become:

- generic rainbow decoration
- purple-blue AI gradient language
- constant RGB distortion
- decorative confetti

### Frontier-Spectrum Violet

Violet, cobalt and magenta may be used inside the landing Frontier Field when they represent unresolved candidate matter before evaluation has made state legible.

This is a field-material exception, not a general palette expansion.

Use frontier-spectrum violet for:

- early-cycle divergence
- unresolved possibility
- interference between competing trajectories
- volumetric depth inside the frontier field
- memory traces before they resolve into measured state

It must transition, compete with, or give way to semantic state colours as the cycle advances:

- product tables
- navigation
- buttons
- metric values
- generic page backgrounds
- broad decorative gradients
- brand wallpaper
- every illustration or diagram

The rule is:

**violet is unresolved frontier material, not Affine's brand colour.**

### Gold

Gold represents validated progress.

Use gold for:

- selected states
- inherited advancement
- champion state
- frontier movement
- resolution moments
- proven value

Gold rules:

- gold must be scarce
- gold appears after validation
- gold is not ornament

## 13. Layout And Product UI

Product surfaces must earn trust before they create awe.

Network pages, miner metrics, benchmark reports and challenge histories should be designed for repeated use by technical users.

They should be:

- scannable
- comparable
- timestamped
- filterable
- numerically precise
- structurally dense
- visually calm

They may include living-field behaviours, but these should clarify state.

Examples:

- a challenger trajectory approaching a champion threshold
- weak candidates losing coherence
- era memory receding behind current values
- a gold boundary shifting after verified selection
- spectral activity increasing during active competition
- composed capabilities becoming a new inherited state

Avoid importing the full landing-page sublime into utilitarian surfaces.

But do import the frontier grammar.

The product should feel like the instrument panel of the frontier, not a second hero page.

### Product Translation Of The Recursive Frontier

Product UI should translate the landing field into operational structures.

| Landing-field behaviour | Product translation |
| --- | --- |
| Spectral strands enter the field | challengers, miners, model candidates, capability submissions |
| Strands bend under shared pressure | benchmark axes, evaluation rubrics, constraint frames |
| Some strands stray or dim | failed attempts, regressed runs, stale participants, low-confidence rows |
| Useful paths reinforce | improving scores, repeated success, composed capability, increased weight |
| One path resolves more clearly | champion state, selected model, leading benchmark value |
| Gold persists as trace | inherited frontier, retained baseline, validated lineage |
| New strands appear | next epoch, open queue, challenger pool, unresolved capacity |

This translation should be visible in:

- tables through row state, lineage, deltas and selected thresholds
- charts through frontier lines, candidate traces, failed paths and inherited baselines
- metric strips through champion, current, prior and pending state separation
- filters through meaningful comparison, not ornamental controls
- empty states through reopening, not blankness

The app should never feel as if the field disappeared after the landing page.

It should feel as if the field became measurable.

### Frame And Field

The interface should distinguish:

**The frame**

- navigation
- controls
- grids
- labels
- thresholds
- benchmark definitions
- identifiers
- timestamps

**The field**

- candidates
- trajectories
- scores
- composition
- competition
- resolution
- memory
- frontier movement

The frame remains stable while the field evolves.

This distinction applies at every scale:

- on the landing page, the frame is the lockup, nav and proof perimeter
- on the network page, the frame is the data architecture around the frontier
- inside a chart, the frame is the axis and threshold system
- inside a table, the frame is the column logic and row hierarchy

When the field becomes visually rich, strengthen the frame.

When the frame becomes dense, let the field appear through small but meaningful state changes.

## 15. Practical Application Rules

This section translates the doctrine into decisions for the current mockup.

Use it when editing `Affine.dc.html`.

### Current Landing Contract

The current landing page should remain:

- full-viewport
- field-led
- immersive
- instrumented
- sparse in copy
- anchored by a small Affine lockup
- supported by live champion and benchmark evidence

Do not reintroduce:

- a large central logo
- an "inspect frontier" pill
- a click-to-open modal replay
- a secondary chart layer that repeats the hero thesis
- decorative particles without alignment behaviour
- a landing layout that feels like a SaaS hero or dashboard

The landing page's job is not to explain everything.

Its job is to make the visitor feel that distributed intelligence is becoming cumulative inside a measurable system.

### Page Roles

Each page should lead with a different expression mode.

**Landing**

Field Mode.

The field carries emotion. The frame carries evidence.

**Network**

Instrumented Field Mode.

Use dense data, champion state, challenger queues, thresholds, history and benchmark evidence. Motion should clarify state, not create ceremony.

This page should feel most like the landing field translated into a serious frontier instrument.

**Mine**

Product Instrument Mode.

Make participation, requirements, environment state and expected action obvious. Avoid theatrical motion that slows comprehension.

**Mechanism**

Instrumented Field Mode with Archive support.

Explain how incentives, evaluation, alignment, resolution and inheritance work. Diagrams should reveal causality.

**Manifesto**

Archive Mode with controlled Field Mode moments.

Use restraint, rhythm and conceptual clarity. Alchemy can be more present here, but never occult.

### Decision Matrix

Before adding or changing a visual, identify its system role.

| Element | Allowed when it represents | Not allowed when it is |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Particles | candidate matter, memory, pressure, failed pathways, validated state | ambient sparkle, AI dust, generic energy |
| Gold | validated progress, inherited state, selected outcome | luxury accent, decoration, universal highlight |
| Motion | divergence, alignment, pressure, reinforcement, resolution, inheritance | spectacle, idle animation, sci-fi mood |
| Symbolic resolution | unresolved symbolic matter briefly resolving into approved language | decorative equations, terminal glitch, unreadable product copy |
| Depth | inspection through the mechanism, frontier scale, candidate field structure | empty atmosphere, tunnel effect, cinematic filler |
| Nature reference | behavioural logic, branching, channel formation, reinforcement | leaves, fungi, brains, DNA, literal ecosystem imagery |
| Alchemy reference | transformation, pressure, crystallisation, material progression | occult symbols, manuscripts, rituals, fantasy sigils |
| Logo | identity seal, lockup, stable mechanism, small anchor | redundant hero object, decorative badge, explanation of the whole system |

### Copy Rules In The Mockup

Prefer short causal statements.

Use copy to clarify the mechanism, not to compensate for unclear visuals.

Prefer:

> Affine makes intelligence compound.

> What proves useful becomes the foundation for what comes next.

> Alignment turns possibility into progress.

Avoid:

> Discover the future of decentralised AI.

> Unlock the power of intelligent collaboration.

> A revolutionary ecosystem for machine learning.

### Practical Red Flags

Pause and revise if a change makes the mockup feel like:

- a generic AI landing page
- a Bittensor dashboard with nicer styling
- a particle wallpaper
- a luxury black-and-gold brand
- a cinematic teaser without product truth
- a product interface that is too theatrical to use repeatedly
- a logo showcase instead of a mechanism

### The Implementation Standard

A good Affine screen should answer, at a glance:

- what is acting independently
- what aligns it
- what pressure it encounters
- what strengthens or disappears
- what becomes inherited
- what evidence proves it

If the screen cannot answer those questions, simplify or restructure it before adding more visual richness.

### Change Protocol

Before changing the mockup, identify the kind of change being made.

**Strategic change**

Changes what Affine means.

Requires updating this document before changing the interface.

Examples:

- changing the operating cycle
- changing the relationship to Bittensor
- changing the role of gold
- changing whether the landing page is field-led

**System change**

Changes how the design language expresses the strategy.

Requires checking Sections 8, 9, 10, 11, 12, 13, 15, 17, 18 and 19.

Examples:

- new field behaviour
- new motion grammar
- new visual treatment for particles
- new product data layout

**Surface change**

Changes a specific page or component without changing the system.

Requires passing the relevant page role and the design test.

Examples:

- changing landing copy
- adjusting benchmark display
- editing the network table
- adding miner metrics

If a surface change repeatedly needs exceptions, it is probably a system change.

If a system change contradicts the defining statement, it is probably a strategic change.

### Page Acceptance Criteria

Use these criteria to decide whether a page is ready.

**Landing passes when:**

- the Frontier Field is the emotional centre
- the Affine lockup anchors identity without competing with the field
- candidate matter feels independent before it aligns
- motion implies pressure, selection, inheritance and reopening
- symbolic resolution, if used, resolves quickly into readable capability language
- gold appears as earned validation, not decoration
- champion and benchmark evidence keep the field credible
- there is no central logo, inspect prompt or modal replay duplicating the hero

**Network passes when:**

- current champion, challenger state and history are immediately legible
- benchmarks match the live frontier logic
- comparison is easier than spectacle
- selected states are distinct from merely positive metrics
- historical memory remains visible
- miner/data-savvy users can scan without fighting the visual system

**Mine passes when:**

- the required action is obvious
- environment status and participation requirements are clear
- incentives feel aligned, not gamified
- motion supports comprehension rather than drama
- the page feels like entering the mechanism, not a marketing funnel

**Mechanism passes when:**

- divergence, alignment, resolution, inheritance and reopening are explainable from the visuals
- diagrams reveal causality
- reward-hacking resistance feels structural
- nature and alchemy remain behavioural, not illustrative
- the page explains why Affine makes intelligence compound

**Manifesto passes when:**

- the language is calm, exact and consequential
- symbolic depth supports product truth
- alchemy gives transformation weight without occult styling
- the mechanism remains more important than mythology

### Final QA Checklist

Before considering a mockup change finished, verify:

- the page still answers the design test
- no large central logo has returned to the landing hero
- no inspect prompt or modal replay has returned to the landing hero
- particles, if present, behave as candidate matter, pressure, memory or validation
- symbolic resolution, if present, is brief, legible at rest and limited to field-led display language
- gold appears only for selected or inherited progress
- the product pages remain usable for repeated technical work
- copy is causal, not hype-driven
- the page does not resemble a generic AI, crypto, gaming, biotech or luxury brand
- mobile and desktop both preserve hierarchy
- visual richness has not hidden evidence

## 19. The Design Test

Every proposed Affine design should answer:

1. What independent actors, capabilities or possibilities are present?
2. What shared field aligns them?
3. What pressure or evaluation changes their behaviour?
4. What weakens, disappears, reinforces or composes?
5. What is validated?
6. What is inherited?
7. How does the next cycle reopen?
8. What evidence keeps the awe honest?
9. Is the symbol acting as useful identity, or redundantly competing with the field?
10. Which expression mode is this using: Field, Instrumented Field, Product Instrument or Archive?
11. Has the frontier grammar been translated at the correct intensity?
12. Does the design make intelligence compounding visible?

A design may be visually impressive and still fail this test.

If these questions cannot be answered, it is not yet Affine.

Create an identity for Affine that expresses intelligence as an emergent but directional process.
